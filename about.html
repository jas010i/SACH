About Me

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a central hub for San Antonio where everyone that has a passion for cars can share that passion. From Bikes to big ole 12" lift with 22's and underglow F-350's, from LS swapped RX-7's to 1500HP 2JZ Supras.

This site will be accepting of all different cultures, opinions and ideas.

My Ride

I have had a lot of different rides on both the JDM side and the American Muscle side. My longest lasting ride was a 2008 Ford Mustang GT. It was full bolt-on and E85 tuned. It has since been sold and I now have a 2014 F-150 Limited Edition VEH. #1841.

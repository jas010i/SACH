My Story

SACH, or San Antonio Car Hub,
was founded April 28th, 2020,
as a project to spruce up my
resume for my Front-End Web
Developer career aspirations.

The website goal is to create
a central hub where everyone in
San Antonio can go to for
information on car clubs, events,
promotions, and even just to see
what other people are driving.

Ideally, this site will become
San Antonio's favorite site for
car-related information.

It's going to be a long journey
to complete this site 100%, but
I'm willing to learn what I must
and apply it to this site!

Here goes nothing!
